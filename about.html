Our Story

Fantastic Nail Salon
reimagined

Fantastic Nail Salon has been a fixture on Highland Avenue for years — a name people in San Bernardino already knew and trusted. Under new ownership, we took that reputation and raised the standard to match it.

The interior was completely reimagined. The process was refined. The team was built around technicians who've spent years developing real skill — not just speed. What stayed the same: the commitment to great prices and a welcoming, calm environment that clients actually want to spend time in.

When you walk through our door on Highland Ave, you'll notice it. The space is clean and considered. The staff takes time to understand what you want. The result is work you'll be proud to show off — not just for a day, but for weeks.

What We Stand For

The three things
we never compromise on

Precision first

We don't rush. A full set gets the same attention on nail ten as it did on nail one. Shaping, prep, application — every step matters to how long it holds and how it looks.

Clean space, calm energy

Our newly redesigned interior is a deliberate choice. Clean, considered, quiet. You should feel relaxed the moment you sit down — not like you're being processed through a conveyor belt.

Honest pricing

High quality doesn't have to mean inflated prices. We're transparent about what everything costs before you sit down, and we deliver results that are worth every dollar — often more.
